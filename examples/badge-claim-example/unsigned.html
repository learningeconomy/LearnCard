Unsigned VC from data-src

Partner hosts an unsigned credential template. LearnCard fetches it, signs it with its issuer DID, and hands the signed VC to the wallet. Simplest partner integration — no signing infrastructure required.

The one line you paste

<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@learncard/embed-sdk@latest/dist/badge-claim.js" async
        data-src="https://learncard.app/demo-badges/accountability-unsigned.json"
        data-mode="both"></script>

Replace data-src with your own public HTTPS URL in production. This demo fixture lives on learncard.app so the claim completes end-to-end without any partner hosting.
