Pre-signed VC from data-src

The partner already signed this credential themselves. LearnCard just wraps it in a Verifiable Presentation at claim time. Best trust model, most portable.

The one line you paste

<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@learncard/embed-sdk@latest/dist/badge-claim.js" async
        data-src="https://learncard.app/demo-badges/accountability-signed.json"
        data-mode="both"></script>

Replace data-src with your own public HTTPS URL in production. This demo points at a fixture hosted on learncard.app so the full claim round-trip completes without any partner hosting.
